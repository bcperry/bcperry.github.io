Revolutionizing field communication with AI-powered wearable technology. Toga converts video and audio streams into concise, text-based logs for efficient, real-time information sharing in critical situations.

Advanced Wearable Data Solution

Introducing Toga, a cutting-edge wearable device that transforms how critical information is captured and communicated in the field. By leveraging state-of-the-art bodycams and actioncams integrated with our proprietary AI solution, Toga efficiently converts visual and audio data into concise, text-based logs optimized for low-bandwidth transmission.

Key Features:

  • Integration with cutting-edge bodycams and actioncams
  • Real-time conversion of video and audio to text-based logs
  • Custom AI solution for efficient data processing
  • Low-bandwidth optimized transmission
  • Seamless integration with existing communication systems

Benefits:

  • Enhanced real-time situational awareness
  • Improved communication in bandwidth-constrained environments
  • Efficient capture and relay of critical information
  • Reduced cognitive load on field personnel
