Laelaps

Named for the hunting dog that always caught its prey, Laelaps is a hardware system designed to convert any unguided rocket into a guided missile tailored for any target. From small Unmanned Arial Systems, to large aircraft, to tanks. Laelaps provides countermeasure resistant guidance to any motor, for any target, on demand.

Key Features

  • Universal compatibility with unguided rockets
  • Countermeasure resistant guidance system
  • Adaptable to various target types
  • On-demand deployment capabilities

Applications

From small Unmanned Aerial Systems to large aircraft and tanks, Laelaps provides versatile guidance solutions for a wide range of military applications.
